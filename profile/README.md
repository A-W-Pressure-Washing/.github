## 👋 Who We Are

**A&W Pressure Washing** is a Middle Tennessee, veteran-owned small business delivering **safe, professional exterior cleaning** with modern, low-pressure/soft-wash techniques. From vinyl siding and roofs to driveways, patios, gutters, and commercial storefronts, we combine **industry best practices** with **customer-first service**.

- ✅ Fully insured
- ♻️ Eco-friendly detergents & runoff awareness
- 🧰 Professional-grade equipment
- 🕒 Reliable scheduling and confirmations

> Want to collaborate on tooling (estimators, booking, or workflow automation)? We maintain public repos and welcome PRs!

## 🧼 Services

- House siding (soft wash)
- Roof cleaning (asphalt shingle/metal)
- Driveways, walkways, patios
- Decks & fences
- Gutter cleaning & brightening
- Commercial storefronts & lots

<details>
  <summary><b>Safety & Method</b></summary>

- **Soft wash** for siding/roofs to avoid damage
- Pre-rinse landscaping, use plant-safe practices
- Correct dilution, dwell, and rinsing times
- Post-treat concrete for organic staining
</details>
